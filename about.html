About Us

FTC Videos is a site dedicated to aggregating and organizing educational and match-related videos for the FIRST Tech Challenge community. We created it to provide an easily accessible and centralized resource for teams, mentors, and enthusiasts looking to learn from past competitions, strategies, and technical tutorials.

At its core, FTC Videos allows users to efficiently browse and watch videos by season, event, and team. Whether you're looking for match footage, robot design inspiration, programming tutorials, or strategy breakdowns, our platform makes it simple to find relevant content. Our goal is to make learning and research for FTC more convenient and accessible to everyone in the community.
